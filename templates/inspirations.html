People that inspire me, and my future aspirations
Who I admire the most

My father. Some of the things that I admire most about my father are his determination and persistence. My grandfather wouldn’t allow him to continue education saying that education is expensive although he was able to afford it, and by the time my father had passed the National Exams. Moreover, His father would sell off my father’s small business activities that he tried to start at home and also beat my grandmother close to death. Therefore, My father left home at seventeen to shape his own life. Not everyone at this age is able to accomplish what he had achieved so far. From building a successful business, having a happy family, being a goodman, and supporting and understanding us in every way possible.

Why do I want to go to college

There are several reasons why I need to attend college. Both of my parents did have a chance to at least to step in highschool. Therefore, as I grew up they always told me how much setbacks they face due to lack of appropriate education. So, by this I grew up knowing the value of education. Moreover, I believe that by attending college I will be able to pursue my passions deeply through gaining additional knowledge and further hands-on experience in the fields that I am passionate about. Finally, I think that interacting with fellow students in several student organisations at college will further contribute to my personal and intellectual growth.

What Do You Expect to Be Doing 10 Years From Now

I wish to be working for or owning a solution oriented company, where I will be working with several other computer scientists using technology to tackle problems facing our community, like pollution control and sustainably improving on certain materials that we use today. Moreover, I wish to make contributions to communities in underdeveloped countries to assist in their development.

Why do you want to go to Vanderbilt university

In my application, I highlighted how I like to know and explore more of the passions that I possess. In addition to being enthusiastic about science and technology, I developed an interest in economic activities when helping my parents to run a business. At Vanderbilt, students are given opportunities to pursue whatever interests them, and this makes me believe that it is the perfect environment for someone like me who needs to forge an interdisciplinary understanding of different things.
